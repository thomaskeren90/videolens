## Winning Format Patterns

**1. "Rahasia" / Secret Formula** — 2-3M views
- Title: "Cara Dapat XXX Tanpa Modal"
- Body: Reveal partial method
- Sell: Full PDF/course via Lynk.ID (Rp 50-500K)

**2. Free Tutorial → Paid Upsell** — 1-2M views
- Title: "[Topic] Pemula Sampe Bisa"
- Body: Free partial guide
- Sell: Full version / template pack

**3. Income Claim Story** — 500K-2M views
- Title: "DAPAT XXX DALAM Y HARI!"
- Body: Screenshot proof + story
- Sell: "The system that made it happen"
